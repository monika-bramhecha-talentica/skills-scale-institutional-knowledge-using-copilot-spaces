# Risk Management — OctoAcme (Playbook)

Purpose
Define how projects should identify, assess, and mitigate risks.

Guidelines
- Use the TEMPLATES/raid-log.md for tracking.
- Classify impact as Low / Medium / High.
- Owners must review risks weekly and update status.

Escalation
- High-impact risks that are unresolved for >5 business days should be escalated to sponsor and PM office hours.
