# PM Hub Governance

Owners
- Each top-level page and playbook must list an owner (individual or role).
- Owners approve content changes and coordinate review cycles.

Review cadence
- Tier A (How we work): quarterly
- Tier B (Playbooks): biannually
- Examples: annually

Deprecation policy
- Mark outdated pages with `DEPRECATED` in front-matter and add a pointer to replacement.
- Keep archives in EXAMPLES/archives.

Maintenance
- Maintainers triage incoming suggestions weekly and clear the backlog monthly.
